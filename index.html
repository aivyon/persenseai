Product concept

The Visual Debug Agent

PersenseAI is exploring a perception diagnostics agent for Computer Vision teams. Upload frames, video clips, pipeline logs, detections, tracks, or evaluation artifacts; the agent inspects the evidence, identifies likely failure modes, and produces a structured diagnostic report.

Instead of treating model reliability as a single score, the agent reasons through what happened: where the track fragmented, why identities crossed, which frames lost confidence, and what a CV engineer should inspect next.

What it can analyze

Built for the places visual systems break.

01

Tracking failures

Track fragmentation, trajectory jumps, missed associations, and unstable motion continuity.

02

ID switches

Identity flips across adjacent frames, crowded scenes, and reappearing objects.

03

ReID mismatches

Embedding confusion, identity collisions, cross-camera appearance variance, and threshold sensitivity.

04

Occlusions

Partial or full visibility loss, re-entry ambiguity, and recovery behavior after hidden intervals.

05

Detection drift

Confidence decay, bounding-box instability, class confusion, and missed low-light detections.

06

Camera transitions

Handoff failures, timing gaps, calibration assumptions, and multi-camera continuity errors.

07

Lighting / domain shift

Scene changes, weather, sensor variance, compression artifacts, and deployment-world mismatch.

08

Evaluation gaps

Metrics that hide frame-level failure patterns, edge cases, and operational reliability risks.

Agent workflow

From visual evidence to engineering next steps.

Observe

Read frames, video, logs, and tracks as one multimodal incident.

Inspect

Locate temporal anomalies, weak detections, identity conflicts, and scene stressors.

Reason

Connect visual symptoms with pipeline components, thresholds, and model behavior.

Diagnose

Rank likely failure modes with concise evidence and affected intervals.

Recommend

Suggest the next checks, audits, tuning experiments, or data slices.

Why it matters

Real-world CV reliability fails between the abstractions.

Production perception systems rarely fail as a clean model problem. They fail between frames, across cameras, inside embeddings, around thresholds, under occlusion, and within deployment constraints. PersenseAI is designed around that messy middle: the practical diagnostic layer between perception research and operating vision systems.
